ABOUT WScarfs

WScarfs is a boutique business that sells trendy headscarves designed for muslimah. Founded by a young talented woman named Wan Nur Adlina Wan Buanan who has a vision to produce a high quality materials of headscarves that will make you stay comfortable everytime you style any WScarfs items. She wants to keep looks of muslimah today to be modesty yet modern with our luxurious designs. WScarfs have been operated since 15th June 2016 at Jalan Putra Square, Kuantan Pahang.

We have produced headscarves for any range of age from kids to woman for them to look gorgeous, cute and mordern. We want to make our brand compete with famous designers or businessman in Malaysia. WScarfs will give 10% promo for first timers customers for any purchases and we will also provide a membership card for those who wants to be members of WScarfs.
